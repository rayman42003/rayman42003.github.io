IronCogGoblin

Instructions

Follow the instructions in game! Use A/D or Left/Right Arrow keys to move the player. Press W/Up Arrow to jump. Use Spacebar to steal items like treasure chests. Use Down to hide in barrels. If you ever get stuck, press R to restart the level.

This is a 2D platformer where you play as a goblin trying to steal treasure. You have to platform your way through traps and sneak past guards to get to the exit. Developed in Unity for the Ludum Dare compo (48 hours solo project).
